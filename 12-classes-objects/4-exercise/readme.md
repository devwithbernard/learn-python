## User Management
   - User registration & authentication (Admin, Librarian, Member)
   - Role-based access control
   - User profile management
## Book Management
   - Add, update, and delete books
   - Book categorization (e.g., Fiction, Non-fiction, Science, History)
   - Book search & filter (by title, author, genre, availability)
## Borrowing & Returning System
   - Book issue & return tracking
   - Due date & overdue fine calculation
   - Renewal and reservation options
   - Notifications/reminders for due dates
## Inventory Management
   - Stock tracking & book availability status
   - Damaged/lost book reports
   - Supplier & purchase management
